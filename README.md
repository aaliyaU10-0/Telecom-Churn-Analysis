# Telecom-Churn-Analysis

## Overview
This project analyzes telecom customer data
to identify key factors contributing to 
customer churn and provide insights to
improve retention.

## Tools Used
- Excel (PivotTables, charts, data analysis)

## Project Structure
The Excel workbook includes:

- **telecom_churn**: Raw dataset used for
  analysis
- **Data_Dictionary**: Defintions and
  descriptions of all variables
- **Pivot Table**: Data summaries used to
  identify churn patterns
- **Visualizations**: Charts illustrating
  trends in churn behavior
- **insights**: Key findings and business recommendations

## Key Insights
- Customers who did not renew contracts had a
  significantly higher churn rate (-42.4%)
  compared to those who renewed (-11.5%)
- Churn increases sharply after multiple
  customer service calls, indicating unresolved
  service issues
- Higher monthly charges are associated with
  increased churn, suggesting pricing
  sensitivity

## Business Recommendations
- Target customers approaching contract
  renewal with retention offers
- Identify high risk customers based on
  service call frequency
- Improve customer service processes to
  reduce repeat calls
- Consider pricing incentives for high risk
  customer segments
